[h1]1.0.0.0: 100%[/h1]
After 6 months of work (alongside a few objects/particles/objects, the two UI mods, and my other super secret work [coming soon!]), all vanilla spell icons have finally been resprited. It's been quite the learning experience for both my art and modding as a whole, and I'm proud to have come this far. Thank you all for taking the time to check out this mod and for your support!

So... what's next?
Well, as you can probably tell, the mod version has gained an extra number; now instead of major.content.patch, it'll look like overhaul.major.content.patch. This is because further updates will contain large amounts of WIP content as the primary focuses change, and respective assets will be added as a 'major' complete release once a primary focus is completed.
The focus will turn from the spells to the environment, with a few other things alongside. I am [i]far[/i] from done here...
